Spotify playlists I made throughout study abroad

Music from my time in Cape Town, Ahmedabad, and Sao Paulo

My study abroad program in the fall of my Junior year focused on global cities in the 21st century. We usually worked and studied at a local university or community center and had to commute to and from our home stays every morning. I made a playlist for each of the three cities we stayed in - Sao Paulo, Cape Town, and Ahmedabad. I would listen to these playlists during my commute and slowly started associating different places with different songs. Once when I got lost in Sao Paulo I tried to listen to my playlist to help me remember how to get home. It didn’t work, but it made being lost a lot more fun.

I also just wanted to learn how to embed playlists into a website, which wasn’t hard at all!
