URBAN GIS: NORTHSIDE ACHIEVEMENT ZONE

In the spring of 2017 I took an Urban GIS course that partnered with the Northside Achievement Zone (NAZ). I worked in the Housing Stability group and focused on gentrification susceptibility, evictions, and shifting homeownership patterns.

“NAZ exists to permanently close the achievement gap and end generational poverty in North Minneapolis. Together with our partner organizations, we are walking side by side with low-income families as they put their children on a path to college.”
